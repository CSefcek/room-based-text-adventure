The princess of the kingdom of CPeria has been captured by an evil wizard and placed in a castle guarded by monsters.
Your job is to set her free and bring her back outside the castle.
The castle has 9 rooms. The game starts with you standing in room 1. Some of the rooms are linked together.
The princess is locked in room 9. There are two monsters in the castle that need to be killed in order to reach the princess.
Will you be able to defeat the monsters and save the princess?
